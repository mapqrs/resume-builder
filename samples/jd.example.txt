Senior Backend Engineer — Payments Platform

About PayCo
We're building the next-generation payments rails for India's small businesses.
Recently launched UPI Lite integration; now scaling to 100M+ monthly transactions.

The role
You'll own a service end-to-end, from API design through observability and on-call.
Expect to work closely with risk, compliance, and product teams.

What you'll do
- Design and ship distributed payment-processing services in Go.
- Own Postgres performance: query plans, partitioning, replication strategy.
- Drive incident response, write post-mortems, improve runbooks.
- Mentor junior engineers and review architecture proposals.

What we're looking for
- 5+ years of backend engineering, ideally on payments or other money-movement systems.
- Strong Go and Postgres skills.
- Distributed systems experience: at-least-once delivery, idempotency, eventual consistency.
- Excellent written communication — you'll write more docs than code in some weeks.
- On-call ownership mindset.

Nice to have
- Familiarity with NPCI/UPI specs or open banking APIs.
- Open-source contributions in the payments or infra space.
- Experience scaling a system from 1M to 50M daily requests.

Location: Bengaluru (hybrid) or remote within India.
